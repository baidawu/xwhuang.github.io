Brief Biography

I am a graduate student in Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) in Master of Science in Computer Science program.

I graduated from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2019. My major was Computer Science and Technology, and I recieved a Bachelor degree of Engineering in USTC. I studied as an assistant student in the NEL-SLIP in Jiajia project team in 2017. I was the TA of Computer Programming in 2018 fall in USTC. Later I worked in the Anhui Province Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Application from 2018 to 2019, and I finished my graduate thesis "An Aided Diagnostic Method for Plant Diseases Based on Image" during the time. My current research interest is Machine Learning.
